About us

Godwin Contracting Services Ltd is an established building and refurbishment contractor offering a range of services: both internal and external refurbishment, fit out, new build and property extensions. Accommodating private and commercial works, carried out in live and occupied environments or with operating restrictions in place, our range of experience enables us to deliver to our client’s expectations.

Working with firm values and a strong family ethos, we believe in constructing a better environment for the future, in the work place throughout commercial projects, in homes with our residential and private operations and a living one with considerate values and responsible work practices when it comes to waste and sourcing of materials.

With three generations of qualitative experience, establishing and enviable reputation in the commercial building trade. We adapt to the changing need of clients either through competitive tender, directly or through partnering, retaining quality and strictly keeping to work schedules.
